02
Page 3 — Editor's letter

Sabr — the Arabic word for patience. Nintai — its Japanese mirror. 忍耐. Both point to the same discipline: a life shaped by what you refuse to rush. Latterdays is the wardrobe of that practice.

We make clothes the way this zine is made: one issue at a time, no hurry. What's here is what waited to be here. Turn the page when you're ready.

— The editors
