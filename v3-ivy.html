LTD 301 · 3 credits

Days of Sabr.

Spring MMXXVI · Instructor of Record: the Pond · 忍耐

Registration open

Description

An advanced seminar in stillness, delivered through a capsule of garments. Students will be asked to wait — for the fabric to settle, the mornings to cool, the work to be finished. Meets one season per year. Enrollment is by letter.

Learning outcomes

  • Re-calibrate the pace of looking.
  • Identify sabr (Arabic) and nintai (Japanese) in the daily wardrobe.
  • Complete one full winter without hurry.
